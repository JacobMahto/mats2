# Preface
1.  This [Manual of Air Traffic Services (MATS) Part 2](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/mats2) is prepared for the use and guidance of executives and staff of AAI at Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru. The manual provides processes, procedures and instructions that are essential for the provision of safe and efficient air traffic services within the airspace under the jurisdiction of Bengaluru ATC.

2.  This Manual has been developed under the guidance of General Manager (ATM), Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru. It contains information, guidance procedures and instructions applicable to the ATS units of Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru and maintained by The General Manager (ATM), Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru.

3.  This manual should be read in conjunction with the following:
    - [ICAO Annex 2 – Rules of the Air](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/anx2)
    - [ICAO Annex 11 – Air Traffic Services](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/anx11)
    - [PANS-ATM Doc 4444 – Procedures for Air Navigation Services – Air Traffic Management and Amendments](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/doc44)
    - [Aeronautical Information Publication (AIP) – India (e-AIP)](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/eaip)
    - [Aircraft Rules 1937](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/rl37)
    - Relevant DGCA CARs and Circulars
    - [Manual of Air Traffic Services – Part 1](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/mats1)
    - [Bengaluru Rating Training Manual (RTM)](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/rtm)
    - CAP Circulars and documents issued by the Directorate of Certification of ATS Personnel (DoCAP)
    - Current Air Traffic Management Circulars (ATMCs)

4. It is to be recognized that in the changing aviation safety environment, amendments to the manual may be required due to:
    - Changes/amendments to ICAO Annexes/Documents
    - Changes/introduction of DGCA CARs
    - Introduction of new technology
    - Requirements from ATS, airlines, or any other concerned agency

5.  This edition of the manual is issued after updating it and incorporating the recent changes and is based on the basis of experience gained and comments/ suggestions received from the users of this manual. Views, comments and suggestions for improvement of this edition, may be sent to the General Manager (ATM), Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru
