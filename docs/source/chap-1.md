# Document Identification and Control

## 1.1 Title

This document is identified as [“MANUAL OF AIR TRAFFIC SERVICES-PART 2”](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/mats2) (Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru)

## 1.2 Purpose

The [Manual of Air Traffic Services Part 2](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/mats2) contains procedures, information and instructions those are applicable to ATS units at Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru. It provides information which amplifies and interprets at local level the instructions contained in [MATS Part 1 Edition 7.0 ATM/SQMS/2025/E7.0-MATS-PRT-1](https://www.eternallyobsessed.com/atc/link/mats1). It is published for the guidance of air traffic controllers to enable them to provide safe and efficient air traffic services at Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru.

## 1.3 Documentation and Publication

The documentation and publication of this Manual is done under the authority of The General Manager (ATM), Airports Authority of India, Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru 560300.

## 1.4 Amendments to Document

The Joint General Manager/ Deputy General Manager (ATM-SQMS), Bengaluru is responsible for making changes, when necessary, to the Manual. Half yearly reviews of the manual are conducted by the GM (ATM) Bengaluru to ensure accuracy and update of all reference data. Amendments to the Manual are published half yearly as replacement pages together with a summary of changes and a checklist of pages.

## 1.5 Effective Date

Effective date of an instruction is indicated at the foot of the page.
